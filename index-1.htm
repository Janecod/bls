A Fully Integrated Suite of Products and Services

The Step ecosystem is a suite of complementary products and services aimed at onboarding more people into crypto. Step’s Portfolio Dashboard is the most used platform in the Solana ecosystem for providing wallet-level data insights. Step also offers onchain data APIs for hobbyists up to large enterprises. SolanaFloor is the #1 media publication on Solana, specifically focused on high-quality journalism in the ecosystem. Built to Reward and Engage

Complimentary Tokenomics Model for the Step Ecosystem

At Step Finance, the STEP token features a unique tokenomics model that rewards users for their engagement. By staking STEP, users receive xSTEP, which entitles them to protocol fees, through STEP that is purchased directly from the open market and distributed to stakers. Every interaction on the platform is designed to enhance the value of the STEP token.
